Community Schools

What is a Community School?

If a student is concerned about their safety, they will be able to learn in school. Many low-income families are extremely concerned with safety. Because of this, low-income students tend to perform worse in school than wealthy students.

Community schools ensure that student safety does not get in the way of learning.

The Community Schools Forward task force has 6 key features that should make up an ideal community school, which are as follows:

"Empowering student and family engagement,
Collaborative leadership, shared power, and voice,
Expanded and enriched learning opportunities,
Integrated systems of supports,
Rigorous, community-connected classroom instruction,
[and] A culture of belonging, safety, and care".
